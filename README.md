RECOMMENDATIONS FOR EXECUTIVE DECISION-MAKING

1. Prioritize High-Demand Vehicle SegmentsIncrease procurement, marketing, and dealership stock levels for SUVs and Hatchbacks, as these body styles are the primary drivers of sales revenue.

2. Optimize Seasonal Sales PlanningImplement pre-Q4 marketing campaigns and inventory buildup to fully capitalize on the strong year-end purchasing cycle.

3. Strengthen Dealer Network PerformanceIdentify operational and sales practices from top-performing dealers and deploy them across the dealership network to improve overall sales performance.

4. Apply Regional Demand ForecastingUse historical sales data to forecast regional demand by body style, ensuring that dealerships receive the most appropriate vehicle mix.

5. Expand Market Reach Through Targeted SegmentationDevelop marketing initiatives aimed at underrepresented customer groups, particularly female buyers, to expand the customer base and unlock additional revenue opportunities.
